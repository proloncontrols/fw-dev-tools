Detailed Description

The memory_walker_stm32 class is a vistor for calculating the CRC used by the hardware CRC unit on the STM32 series of ARM MPUs.

The algo used by the STM32 hardware unit is just a CRC with a different polynomial and word-fed instead of byte-fed. The MPU is little endian but srecord will compute the correct checksum regardless of the endianness of the generating platform. http://www.st.com/
